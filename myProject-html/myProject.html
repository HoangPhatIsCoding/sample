Overview about Vietnam

Vietnam is a vibrant Southeast Asian country known for its stunning landscapes, rich culture, and tumultuous history. With Hanoi as its capital and Ho Chi Minh City as its largest urban center, the nation boasts a diverse geography ranging from mountainous regions to beautiful beaches. Renowned for its delicious cuisine, including pho and banh mi, Vietnam's culture reflects influences from Chinese and French traditions. The country has seen significant economic growth in recent decades and is a popular tourist destination, famous for sites like Ha Long Bay and the ancient town of Hoi An. [more]
